Current Research

Universal ReID

Reliability-Aware 3D Geometric Injection for Universal Person Re-identification

ECCV 2026 submission on reliability-aware 2D–3D feature fusion, SMPL-based geometry representation, and multi-benchmark evaluation.

  • Reliability-aware 2D–3D feature fusion
  • SMPL-based geometry representation
  • Occlusion, clothing change, and cross-modal robustness
  • Unified training and ablation across 9 benchmarks

Scientific Graph Extraction

GraphLite: High-Quality Supervision Enables Deployable Small Models for Graph Extraction

Ongoing work on scientific graph extraction using ARCHE-derived supervision, aimed at deployable small-model learning.

  • Supervision construction from ARCHE-derived signals
  • Evaluation workflow validation
  • 0.8B-scale training pipeline setup
  • Planned submission to SDM

ReID Survey

Survey and Metric Design for Appearance-Variant Person Re-identification

Ongoing survey work on ReID under clothing, illumination, and pose changes, targeting TPAMI survey preparation.

  • Task taxonomy and method organization
  • Evaluation limitations in real-world appearance variation
  • Metric design and benchmark interpretation
